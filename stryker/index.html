My 9-Month Strategic Execution Roadmap

A phased approach to establish market presence, accelerate sales, and build lasting client relationships.

Q1: Foundation & Sales Blitz (Months 1-3)

**Objective:** Rapidly onboard, understand the market, and immediately initiate targeted sales efforts to generate early wins and build pipeline momentum.

Month 1: Immersion & Immediate GTM

  • **Day 1-5: Deep Dive & Onboarding:** Full immersion into Beyond's platform, features, and existing client case studies. Shadowing product/dev teams.
  • **Week 1-2: Initial Market Mapping & ICP Definition:**
    • Identify Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) based on initial understanding (e.g., growing SMEs, family-owned businesses, professional services, manufacturing).
    • Analyze high-level industry pain points (meeting productivity, action tracking, strategy execution).
  • **Week 2-4: Rapid Prospecting & Outreach Launch:**
    • **Immediate List Building:** Leverage LinkedIn Sales Navigator and initial data enrichment tools to build a first wave of qualified prospect lists.
    • **Personalized Cold Outreach:** Launch targeted, pain-point-driven email sequences and LinkedIn messages. Focus on booking initial discovery calls.
    • **First Discovery Calls:** Begin conducting discovery calls, focusing on active listening and identifying immediate pain points where Beyond can provide quick wins.
  • **Tools Setup:** CRM (e.g., Salesforce/HubSpot), Sales Engagement Platform (e.g., Outreach.io/Salesloft), LinkedIn Sales Navigator, Data Enrichment Tools (e.g., ZoomInfo/Apollo.io).

Month 2: Pipeline Building & Audit Initiation

  • **Aggressive Lead Generation:** Continue targeted list building and multi-channel outreach campaigns.
  • **AI-Automated Lead Scoring:** Implement AI-driven lead scoring within CRM to prioritize prospects based on engagement and fit, ensuring focus on high-potential opportunities.
  • **Qualification & Audit Framework Introduction:** Rigorous qualification of leads. Introduce the "AI Audit" framework early in the sales cycle to systematically uncover client needs and align them with Beyond's solutions.
  • **Initial Audit Presentations:** Conduct first audits, demonstrating Beyond's understanding of their business challenges and potential solutions.

Month 3: Early Wins & Process Refinement

  • **Proposal Development & Presentation:** Develop and present tailored proposals based on audit findings, focusing on clear ROI.
  • **Negotiation & Closing:** Actively engage in negotiations, focusing on value-based selling. Aim for initial pilot programs or smaller deals to secure early wins.
  • **Feedback Loop:** Collect feedback from initial interactions (both wins and losses) to continuously refine sales process, messaging, and target ICP.
  • **KPI Focus:** Number of discovery calls booked, audits initiated, initial proposals submitted, and crucially, **first deals closed.**

Q2: Engagement & Conversion Acceleration (Months 4-6)

**Objective:** Deepen client engagement, refine the sales process, and drive initial conversions by clearly demonstrating ROI.

Month 4: Tailored Solution & Proposal Development

  • **Deep Dive Audit & Analysis:** Conduct detailed follow-up sessions based on initial audit findings. Use Beyond's internal tools to map client workflows.
  • **Value Proposition Customization:** Tailor Beyond's features to specific client pain points, demonstrating how "Meetings That Matter" or "Track Actions, Not Promises" directly solve their issues (e.g., for a manufacturing client, how Beyond streamlines production meetings and tracks shop floor actions).
  • **Proposal Crafting:** Develop bespoke proposals, including clear ROI projections and implementation roadmaps. Leverage AI for data-driven ROI calculations.
  • **Interactive Presentation Development:** Create dynamic HTML presentations for each key prospect, visually articulating the value.

Month 5: Negotiation & Closing Strategies

  • **Objection Handling Workshop:** Proactively identify and prepare for common sales objections, focusing on Beyond's integration capabilities and ease of adoption.
  • **Value-Based Negotiation:** Focus discussions on the value Beyond delivers, not just cost. Utilize case studies and testimonials.
  • **Trial/Pilot Programs:** Offer structured pilot programs for key prospects to experience Beyond's benefits firsthand, with clear success metrics.
  • **Closing Techniques:** Implement effective closing strategies, ensuring all stakeholders are aligned.

Month 6: Onboarding Handoff & Initial Success

  • **Seamless Handoff:** Ensure smooth transition of new clients to the onboarding/customer success team.
  • **First Success Stories:** Work closely with customer success to identify and document initial client wins and positive feedback.
  • **Feedback Loop:** Collect feedback from closed/lost deals to continuously refine sales process and messaging.
  • **KPI Focus:** Number of proposals submitted, pilot programs initiated, deals closed, average deal size.

Q3: Relationship Nurturing & Expansion (Months 7-9)

**Objective:** Cultivate long-term client relationships, drive adoption, identify expansion opportunities, and generate referrals.

Month 7: Account Management & Value Reinforcement

  • **Regular Check-ins:** Schedule proactive check-ins with existing clients to ensure satisfaction and identify new use cases.
  • **Value Reviews:** Conduct quarterly business reviews (QBRs) showcasing the ROI Beyond has delivered for their business using data from Beyond's scorecards.
  • **Problem Solving:** Act as a strategic advisor, helping clients leverage Beyond to solve evolving challenges.
  • **Feedback Integration:** Gather client feedback on product features and relay to product team for continuous improvement.

Month 8: Upselling, Cross-selling & Referrals

  • **Identify Expansion Opportunities:** Based on client success, identify opportunities to upsell additional Beyond features or cross-sell to other departments within the client organization.
  • **Referral Program Activation:** Encourage satisfied clients to provide testimonials and referrals.
  • **Case Study Development:** Collaborate with marketing to develop compelling case studies from successful client implementations.
  • **Industry Events:** Represent Beyond at relevant industry events and conferences, leveraging client success stories.

Month 9: Strategic Review & Future Planning

  • **Performance Review:** Comprehensive review of Q1-Q3 performance against KPIs.
  • **Market Insights Contribution:** Provide insights from client interactions and market trends to inform Beyond's product roadmap and sales strategy for the next year.
  • **Strategic Account Planning:** Develop detailed plans for key accounts to maximize lifetime value.
  • **KPI Focus:** Client retention rate, upsell/cross-sell revenue, number of referrals, client satisfaction scores.

A Day in the Life of Joe Ward: On the Road to Growth

A typical day balancing proactive outreach, client engagement, and strategic planning, all powered by smart tools.

7:00 AM - 8:00 AM: Strategic Start

Review Beyond's internal 'Meeting Scorecards' for yesterday's internal meetings. Check CRM for high-priority 'nudge' alerts on off-track actions from key prospects. Plan top 3 priorities for the day.

**Tools:** Beyond Platform (Meeting Scorecards, Action Tracking), CRM.

8:00 AM - 10:00 AM: Prospecting & AI-Powered Outreach

Leverage LinkedIn Sales Navigator to identify new ICPs. Use data enrichment tools to pull contact info. Launch personalized email sequences via Sales Engagement Platform, integrating AI-generated subject lines for optimal open rates.

**Tools:** LinkedIn Sales Navigator, Data Enrichment Tool, Sales Engagement Platform (with AI features).

10:00 AM - 12:00 PM: Discovery & Audit Session (e.g., New Client)

Conduct a virtual discovery call. Actively listen to uncover pain points related to meeting productivity and strategic alignment. Initiate a sample "AI Audit" using the Beyond framework, demonstrating how Beyond can provide clarity and control.

**Tools:** Video Conferencing (Zoom/Google Meet), Beyond Audit Framework, Digital Whiteboard.

12:00 PM - 1:00 PM: Lunch & Learning

Quick lunch. Catch up on industry news and trends. Review Beyond's internal knowledge base for new product updates or sales collateral.

**Tools:** Industry News Feeds, Beyond Knowledge Base.

1:00 PM - 3:00 PM: Proposal Development & Tailored Presentation

Craft a bespoke proposal for a qualified prospect. Use an interactive HTML template to customize the presentation, embedding relevant charts and specific benefits for their business. Focus on clear ROI.

**Tools:** CRM (for client data), Custom HTML Templates, Beyond Case Studies.

3:00 PM - 4:30 PM: Follow-up & Nurturing

Follow up on outstanding proposals and active pipelines. Send personalized 'nudge' messages through Beyond's system or CRM to keep conversations alive and address any blockers.

**Tools:** CRM, Beyond Platform ('Nudge' feature), Sales Engagement Platform.

4:30 PM - 5:00 PM: Daily Review & Planning

Update CRM with all activities. Review daily KPIs. Plan tasks for tomorrow, ensuring alignment with weekly and monthly targets. Document any new insights from client interactions.

**Tools:** CRM, Beyond Platform (Action Tracking, KPI Dashboard).
